    Research interests:
    My research centers on understanding pathways to academic success for students of all demographic and socioeconomic backgrounds with a focus on postsecondary success in engineering fields.

     

    Research approaches:
    My research approaches heavily involve causal identification with observational data and quantitative methods with longitudinal datasets.
